excerpt: "We present the first observation of coherent ion-cyclotron-range density fluctuations (ICE) in Ohmic plasmas on the MAST-U tokamak, where energetic particle drive is expected to be negligible. Using multi-channel Doppler Backscattering diagnostics, we show that these fluctuations correspond to long-lived, high-wave number electrostatic ion cyclotron waves extending across a broad radial region of the plasma edge. Parametric studies reveal a strong dependence of ICE stability on plasma safety factor and elongation, with instability dependence on safety factor."
